Mixed Methods · Client Project

AI Tool Adoption & Professional Identity

Work, Identity & Wellbeing Lab  ·  Michigan Medicine Office of Wellbeing
Advised by Dr. Mari Kira  ·  Ongoing

A qualitative study examining how DAX, an AI clinical transcription tool, affects physicians' professional identity and daily workflows at Michigan Medicine. The study compared active users who continued using the tool with lapsed users who abandoned it, to understand what drives adoption and what causes drop-off.
